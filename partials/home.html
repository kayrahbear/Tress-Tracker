Welcome to Tress Tracker

A lightweight application that allows women experiencing hair loss to query a database of 600+ medical grade wigs and partials from companies like Jon Renau, Raquel Welch, and many more. View each wig's photos, average web price, description, colors, video reviews, and more.

Keep track of what wigs you own, wigs you've tried on in-store, and add pieces to your wish list all within Tress Tracker. Read through helpful resources explaining colors, cap types, and more.
